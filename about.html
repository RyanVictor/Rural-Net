ABOUT US

RuralNet is driven by a shared mission to connect people. The way we connect people is by using leading technologies that deliver quality and efficiency, this enables us to consistently deliver our services to our environment.

​ RuralNet is a serviceprovider that facilitates the Kenyan domestic market. We have a solution for all small and medium business needs. We understand the domestic market like no other and have solutions to face the challenges of doing business on the countryside.
